Compliance Notes

Campaign A — HV Department

  • NHVR claim scoped to Brake Shaker Test Unit only — confirmed in SoT
  • Authorised agent claim (Mack/UD/Volvo) confirmed in SoT. Copy says "authorised agent" only — no warranty safety implied
  • Fleet percentage (~90%) avoided in all copy. "Trusted by" framing used instead — confirm with Jye before adding specific figures
  • No tuning/ECU remapping referenced. Warranty blacklist not triggered
  • No outcome claims, no fake urgency, no before/after imagery

Campaign B — Open Day

  • Triple M Fraser Coast: confirm they're comfortable being named in paid ads before running B3 or CB1 card 2
  • Precision Hydraulics: confirm partner clearance before naming in paid ads
  • Ball Bearing Bonanza / Dyno Tune: copy uses past tense throughout. Clean.
  • New vs expanded facility: confirm with Jye — if expansion not new build, update "new facility" to "expanded facility" across B5 and CB1
  • Children in photos (Core Diesel hoodies): do not use in paid ads without parental consent

Before generating any AI imagery

  • Brand orange #E8631A inferred from photography — confirm exact hex with Jye/Bronte before generating. Update all prompts if different.
  • Fonts: prompts reference "Barlow Condensed Bold" as a display match. Confirm the actual font used on Core Diesel signage.
  • All quoted on-image text strings are compliant as written — if edited in Canva/Figma, recheck against the blacklist before publishing
